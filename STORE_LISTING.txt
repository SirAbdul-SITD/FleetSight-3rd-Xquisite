Full description:
---------------------------------------------------------------
Fleetsight is a ship-placement logic puzzle in the spirit of Battleship.

A fleet of straight-line ships is hidden in the grid. The number beside
each row and above each column tells you how many ship cells sit in that
line. A handful of cells are already revealed as ship or water. Work the
crossings between rows and columns — remembering that ships never touch,
not even diagonally — and the one true fleet reveals itself.

There is no guessing. Each of the 150 puzzles has been checked by a real
solver to guarantee a single, logical fleet layout — a path of pure
deduction always exists from the first cell to the last.

FEATURES
• 150 hand-verified puzzles across three charts
   – Coastal Waters (6×6), Open Sea (8×8), Deep Ocean (9×9)
• Guaranteed unique solution on every board — solvable by logic alone
• Simple tap controls: cycle a cell between ship, water, and blank
• Live conflict detection for touching ships and row/column overflow
• Earn up to three stars per puzzle for efficient solving
• Naval-chart visuals with brass clue numerals and steel-grey hulls
• Calm sonar-toned ambient soundtrack and tactile feedback — both
  toggleable
• Fully offline. No ads, no accounts, no tracking. Progress stays on your
  device.

Find the one true fleet.
